OUR SERVICES

  • OUT-PATIENT CARE SERVICES

  • SOUTHWIND MEDICAL HOSPITAL has accredited more than 10,000 physicians who are ready to provide outpatient consultation for non-emergency cases in hospitals/clinics nationwide. Aside from consultation during regular clinic hours with affiliated physicians/specialists, the following are also provided in accordance with the type of plan acquired:

    • Referrals to affiliated specialists
    • Treatment of minor injuries
    • Surgery not requiring confinement including cataract extraction (except cost of lens)
    • Pre-natal and post-natal consultation with accredited OB-Gynecologists
    • Cauterization of Warts
    • Diagnostic laboratory procedures including x-rays, CT Scans, MRI and other complex examinations
    • Hemodialysis
    • Physical Therapy, Speech Therapy, Occupational therapy
    • Radiation therapy
    • Orthopedic casting and removal
    • Outpatient endoscopic procedures
    • Outpatient laparoscopic procedures

  • IN-PATIENT / HOSPITALIZATION CARE SERVICES

  • SOUTHWIND MEDICAL HOSPITAL has more than 300 hospitals nationwide that are ready to provide care for patients whose medical problem requires confinement or close monitoring. Depending on the type of plan appropriated the following in-patient services may apply when hospitalization is deemed medically necessary:

    • No deposit upon admission
    • Room and Board according to type of enrollment
    • Use of operating, delivery and recovery rooms
    • Services of Medocare accredited specialists
    • Type of anesthesia deemed necessary in a surgical procedure
    • Blood transfusions and Intravenous fluids including cost of blood products
    • Complex and non-complex diagnostic examinations
    • Administered medicines
    • Dressings, plaster casts, sutures and other items directly related to the management of the patient
    • ICU confinement, Chemotherapy, radiation therapy, physical and speech therapy, dialysis
    • All other items related to the management of the illness or
      • injuries

  • EMERGENCY CARE SERVICES

  • A PATIENT may need emergency care if he/she is suffering from severe pain, or when his/her physical condition indicates imminent loss of life and/or limb and other bodily function if treatment is not provided within 6-8 hours from occurrence of the condition. In such cases, we have accredited more than 500 hospitals/clinics nationwide who are ready to provide the following free of charge:

    • Doctor’s services
    • Medicines used during treatment given in the emergency room
    • Oxygen and intravenous fluids
    • Dressings, casts and sutures
    • Laboratory, x-ray and other diagnostic examinations directly related to the emergency management of the patient
    • Use of anti-rabies vaccines, anti-tetanus vaccines, and anti-venom in accordance to the type of plan acquired.

  • LATEST MODALITIES OF TREATMENT

  • A SOUTHWIND MEDICAL HOSPITAL is continuously updating the medical services available to our members in close conjunction with the introduction of globally accepted modern innovations in the medical field. These latest modalities in diagnostics and treatment present in most of our affiliated hospitals/clinics may be availed by our members when deemed necessary by our affiliated physicians and in accordance to the type of plan acquired. These modalities include, but not limited to the following:

    • Phacoemulsification
    • Laparoscopic surgeries
    • Endovenous laser treatments
    • Robotic surgery
    • Stem Cell Treatment
    • Hysteroscopic surgeries
    • Capsule endoscopy
    • Genome testing
    • Other latest modalities offered in the market that are officially accepted by appropriate Medical Societies
